Emory Andrew Tate III (born 1 December 1986) is an American and British social media personality, businessman, and former professional kickboxer. He gained notoriety for promoting various positions in the manosphere community.[3] His controversial commentary has resulted in his expulsion from various social media platforms and concern that he promotes misogynist views to his audience.[4] A divisive influencer,[5] Tate has amassed 9.9 million followers on Twitter as of August 2024[6][7] and was the third-most googled person in 2023,[8] with most British adults aware of who he is.[9] He has been dubbed the "king of toxic masculinity",[10] has called himself a misogynist,[11] and is politically described as both right-wing[12] and far-right.[13] As of August 2024, Tate is facing five legal investigations—three criminal and two civil—in Romania and the United Kingdom
